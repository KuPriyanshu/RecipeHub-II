Gourmet Cheeseburger

A juicy beef patty with melted cheese, fresh veggies, and a soft toasted bun.

Ingredients

  • 200g Ground Beef
  • 1 Brioche Bun
  • 1 Slice Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 Tomato (Sliced)
  • 1 Lettuce Leaf
  • 1 tbsp Mayonnaise
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. 2Season ground beef with salt and pepper, then form a patty.
  2. Heat a pan and cook the patty for 3-4 minutes per side.
  3. Add cheddar cheese on top and let it melt.
  4. Toast the brioche bun in the pan.
  5. Spread mayonnaise on the bun, add lettuce, tomato, and patty.
  6. Top with the other half of the bun and serve.
